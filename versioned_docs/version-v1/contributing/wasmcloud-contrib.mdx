The [`wasmCloud/contrib`](https://github.com/wasmCloud/contrib) GitHub repository collects community contributions to the wasmCloud ecosystem including components and capability providers, demos, deployment scripts, and more. [Browse the repo](https://github.com/wasmCloud/contrib) to explore examples of contributions.

### Who can contribute to `wasmCloud/contrib`?

Anyone! The project welcomes contributions from all wasmCloud users and community members.

### What kinds of projects are appropriate for `wasmCloud/contrib`?

Examples of wasmCloud applications, components, capability providers, deployment scripts, secrets backends, and other open source extensions to wasmCloud are eligible for inclusion in the repo. All contributions must adhere to the CNCF Code of Conduct. The wasmCloud maintainers reserve the right to decline a contribution if it does not align with the Code of Conduct or overall goals of the project. 

We welcome a wide variety of projects and are eager to see what you're making with wasmCloud! If you're unsure whether your idea is a good fit for the repository, you can reach out in the [wasmCloud Slack](https://slack.wasmcloud.com/). 

### Conventions for pull requests

To contribute, simply make a pull request to the [`wasmCloud/contrib` repo](https://github.com/wasmCloud/contrib). 

* Create a directory in the repo for your contribution.
* Sign off on your commit [using the `--signoff` argument](https://git-scm.com/docs/git-commit#Documentation/git-commit.txt---signoff) with your commit.
